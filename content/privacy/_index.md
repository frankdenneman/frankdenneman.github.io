## Introduction

Your privacy matters. This privacy policy explains how **frankdenneman.nl** (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit this website.

By using this website, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Data We Collect

### 1. Anonymous Website Analytics

We use **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to understand how visitors interact with the site. GA4 collects anonymous usage data such as:

- Pages you visit
- Time spent on pages
- Referrer URLs
- Device/browser information
- Anonymous user identifiers

This information does *not* personally identify you. It helps us improve site content and experience.

The GA4 tracking ID used on this site is: **G-XSZJ05LPXJ**.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking via browser extensions or by disabling third-party cookies.

### 2. Log Files

Like most websites, the server may automatically log your IP address, browser type, ISP, date/time stamp, and pages accessed. This data is used for system administration and performance monitoring only.

## Cookies and Tracking

This site may use **cookies** or similar technologies to:

- Enable basic site functionality
- Store site preferences
- Track usage through Google Analytics

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings preferences. You can disable or block cookies if you prefer.

## Third-Party Services

The site includes tracking scripts provided by:

- **Google Analytics (GA4)** — https://analytics.google.com/

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and practices. We encourage you to review them.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure. You use this website at your own risk.

## Children’s Privacy

frankdenneman.nl does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

## Your Choices

If you do not want your usage information to be tracked:

- Use browser settings to block cookies
- Use privacy extensions (e.g., uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger)
- Opt-out of Google Analytics

We may update this policy from time to time. The revised date will appear at the top of this page.
