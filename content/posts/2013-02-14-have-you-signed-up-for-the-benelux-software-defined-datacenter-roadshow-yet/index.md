In less than 3 weeks time, the Benelux Software Defined Datacenter Roadshow starts. Industry-recognized experts from both IBM and VMware share their vision and insights on how to build a unified datacenter platform that provides automation, flexibility and efficiency to transform the way you deliver IT. Not only can you attend their sessions and learn how to abstract, pool and automate your IT services, the SDDC roadshow provides you to meet the expert, sit down and discus technology. The speakers and their field of expertise: **VMware** [Frank Denneman](https://twitter.com/frankdenneman "twitter frank denneman") – Resource Management Expert [Cormac Hogan](https://twitter.com/VMwareStorage "Twitter Cormac Hogan") – Storage Expert [Kamau Wanguhu](https://twitter.com/Borgcube_ "Twitter Kamau Wanguhu") – Software Defined Networking Expert [Mike Laverick](https://twitter.com/Mike_Laverick "Twitter Mike Laverick") – Cloud Infrastructure Expert [Ton Hermes](https://twitter.com/TonHermes "Twitter Ton Hermes") – End User Computing Expert IBM Tikiri Wanduragala – IBM PureSystems Expert Dennis Lauwers – Converged Systems Expert [Geordy Korte](https://twitter.com/gekort "Twitter Geordy Korte") – Software Defined Networking Expert [Andreas Groth](https://twitter.com/andreasgroth "Twitter Andreas Groth") – End User Computing Expert The roadshow is held in three different countries: Netherlands – [IBM forum](http://www-05.ibm.com/nl/clientcenter/contact.html "IBM forum in Amsterdam") in Amsterdam - March 5th 2013 Belgium – [IBM forum in Brussels](http://www-05.ibm.com/be/clientcenter/contact.html "IBM forum in Brussels") - March 7th 2013 Luxembourg – March 8th 2013 The Software Defined Datacenter Roadshow is a full day event and best of all it is free! **[Sign up now](http://info.vmware.com/content/EMEA_20696_LP "Sign up for the Benelux SDDC roadshow")**!
